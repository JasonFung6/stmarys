About

Formed in 1997 from Church members and the remnants of the disbanded local village side, Welwyn St Mary's C of E FC is now in its twenty-second season. The team was initially led by Charles Eggington (Chairman) who remains an integral part of the club set up as well as player / manager Steve Veasey who remained with the club for ninteen years before retiring.

After Five years of playing in friendly matches, St Mary's joined the East Herts and West Essex Church league in 2002, winning back-to-back league titles in 2002-3 and 2003-4. St Mary's continued to challenge across multiple fronts during the years that followed including an incredible National Cup run in the 2007-09 season as the club played matches all over the country to eventually finish as runners up. As two leagues merged to form the Hertfordshire and Borders Church League in 2009, St Mary's had to wait almost eight years for further success as they won the league and league cup double in the 2016-2017 season.
